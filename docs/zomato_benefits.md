# Zomato: Employee Benefits & Stipends

## Period Leaves
Zomato offers 10 days of "Period Leave" per year for all women and transgender employees to foster an inclusive work environment.

## Learning Budget
Every full-time employee receives an annual "Upskilling Grant" of ₹40,000. This can be used for Data Science certifications, AWS/GCP credits, or technical workshops.

## Relocation Support
For those moving to our Gurgaon (HQ) or Bangalore offices, we provide a 15-day stay at a company-partnered hotel and a relocation allowance of up to ₹60,000.

## Meal Credits
Employees receive daily "Zomato Credits" worth ₹200, which can be used for ordering lunch via the Zomato app while working from the office.
