# Zomato: Sustainability & ESG Goals

## Plastic-Free Deliveries
We have a target to 100% transition to plastic-neutral packaging by the end of 2026. Currently, 85% of our high-volume restaurant partners have switched to compostable containers.

## Electric Vehicle (EV) Fleet
Under the "EV100" initiative, Zomato aims to have a 100% electric delivery fleet by 2030. We currently provide a **₹5,000 monthly subsidy** to delivery partners who switch from petrol bikes to EVs.

## Food Waste Reduction
Through our "Feeding India" initiative, we have redirected over **5 million kilograms** of surplus food from restaurants to underprivileged communities across 100 cities in India.
