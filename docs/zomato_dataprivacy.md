# Zomato: Data Governance & Privacy

## Customer PII
Phone numbers and exact house addresses are masked for delivery partners. Only the last 4 digits of a phone number are visible in the partner app.

## Data Retention
User search history for food items is stored for 180 days to power the "Recommended for You" engine. After 180 days, data is anonymized for long-term trend analysis.

## AI Ethics
Our "Feeding India" donation prompts are randomized to avoid "dark patterns" or coercive UI design. We prioritize transparent user choice.

## Technical Stack
Our Data Science team primarily uses Python 3.10+, PyTorch for neural networks, and Snowflake as our primary data warehouse for analytical queries.
