# Zomato: Engineering Culture & Deep Work

## The "Point-One" Principle
We believe in 0.1% daily improvement. Engineers are encouraged to ship small, incremental updates to the consumer app rather than waiting for massive monthly releases.

## Focus Time
To ensure high-quality code for our delivery routing algorithms, Tuesdays and Thursdays are "Silent Mornings." No internal meetings are allowed before 1:00 PM IST.

## Blinkit Integration
Our quick-commerce arm, Blinkit, follows a "10-minute delivery" goal. Any tech debt affecting the picker-app latency must be addressed with P0 priority.

## Gig Partner Welfare
We maintain a "Rider First" approach. Our algorithms must prioritize safety over speed, ensuring delivery partners are not penalized for traffic delays.
